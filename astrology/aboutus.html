get to know us better!

We are inspired by evolutionary astrology and the law of attraction. We see astrology as a framework for abstract energy patterns and themes in one's life. These energy patterns are often expressed differently from individual to individual depending on one's level of awareness and desire to utilize them.

We don’t believe there’s a one-size-fits-all approach to astrology. What we believe is that the individual is always in control. We see the positives in all influences and believe astrology can be an excellent platform to become acquainted with energy, intuition, and self-mastery.

Ultimately, our core goal is to deliver positivity and inspiration to a global audience through the vector of astrology.
